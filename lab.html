Blood Pressure

Standard Range

There are five ranges of blood pressure and each means different status of your heart. The first number is your systolic blood pressure and that is how much pressure your blood is exerting against your artery walls. The second number diastolic blood pressure is pressure when your heart is resting.

To improve your blood pressure readings you must identify which range you fall in. For readings indicate you fall within Hypertension Stage 1 to improve this you must adopt a healthier lifestyle with a mix of leafy greens and onions to improve heart health, and cardiovascular exercises.
